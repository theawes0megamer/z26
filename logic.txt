--- ZERO2SIXTY BOX LOGIC ---
- USES ACCELEROMETER & GPS TO MEASURE TIME
- STARTS MEASURING TIME WHEN ACCELEROMETER DETECTS >0.8GS
- USES GPS TO KNOW WHEN IT HITS 60MPH & 1/4 MILE ETC...

- CODED IN PYTHON USING TKINTER
- GPS CONNECTED BY A SERIAL PORT 
- ACCELEROMETER CONNECTED BY A DIFFERENT SERIAL PORT
- HAS A 1024 X 600 DISPLAY
- HAS A GUI SHOWING 0-60 & QUARTER MILE TIME, CURRENT SPEED, ETC...
- MOCK UI IN UI/
- UI REFRESHES AT 60 FPS, AND GPS REFRESHES AT 10HZ
